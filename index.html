WHAT WE DO?

Depeche Marine and Construction was established in 2003 and had 13 years of experience in many aspects of Glass installation works, Manpower support and Electrical works in buildings and offices. With the ability to handle different scale projects, we are able to provide quality maintenance and operational services to our valued clients. Our committed staff can deliver services and meet our clients’ requirements promptly and efficiently.

It is our top priority to constantly strive to provide only the best services to you. Your satisfaction is recognition of our competence and a testimony of our success.
